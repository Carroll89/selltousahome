# Pennsylvania Citation Library

> **For Scribe:** Use these pre-verified URLs when citing sources in Pennsylvania market pages.
> Last verified: April 2026. Re-check annually or when adding new markets.

---

## State Government

- **PA Dept of Revenue** (transfer tax, inheritance tax): https://www.revenue.pa.gov
- **PA real estate disclosure law** — Title 68 (Real and Personal Property): https://www.legis.state.pa.us
  - Note: legis.state.pa.us blocks automated requests (403) but the site is live. Use as citation — works for human visitors.
- **PA Housing Finance Agency** (PHFA — affordable housing, loan programs): https://www.phfa.org
- **PA Attorney General** (consumer protection, real estate fraud): https://www.attorneygeneral.gov

---

## Federal / National

- **NAR** (National Association of Realtors — research & statistics): https://www.nar.realtor/research-and-statistics
- **Census Bureau** (housing data, population, demographics): https://www.census.gov/topics/housing.html
- **HUD** (fair housing, FHA resources): https://www.hud.gov
- **ATTOM Data** (foreclosure stats, distressed property data): https://www.attomdata.com
